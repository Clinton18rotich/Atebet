## What is Atebet?

**Atebet** is not a language app. It is a digital *kokwet* — a Kipsigis homestead where language, culture, law, and spirit are one unbroken fabric.

The Kipsigis word *pitet* means law, custom, and nature simultaneously. The sun rising is *pitet*. A flower blossoming is *pitet*. Following the ways of the ancestors is *pitet*. There is no separation between natural law and human law. To break custom is *sogorge* — to act unnaturally — and it brings its own consequence.

This app does not teach Kipsigis as a code to be cracked. It cultivates *pitet* through the daily practice of speech.

## The Learning Path as Life Cycle

1. **Onboarding — Spirit Identification:** The user receives a Kipsigis birth name through the sneeze ritual (*Kurenet* and *Kainetap Musarek*)
2. **Novice — Pre-Initiate:** Grammar and core vocabulary. The user is a child of the language
3. **Intermediate — Ketienchin (Instruction):** The *Tumdo* initiation story track. Learning proper behaviour, proverbs, the warrior code, sacred directions
4. **Advanced — Kayaet (River Immersion):** Capstone — recording the full self-introduction, participating in the *Kaberuret* blessing
5. **Adult — Piikab Kutit:** Fully initiated. A *Salanyat* praise name is earned. Contribution to community begins

## Sacred Design Principles

- **East is health, life, prosperity (*Sopondo*):** The opening screen always faces East. All ceremonies in the app face East
- **The fire is continuity:** A missed day dims the hearth. The ultimate curse *"Mein mat"* (May your fire die) is never used lightly
- **The August Restriction (*Robtoe*):** No new skills begin in August. A time for reflection and consolidation
- **Gendered ceremonial knowledge** is presented with cultural context and opt-in respect, never gamified
- **The *Mumek* oath** inspires our contributor integrity system — speaking truth for the ancestors

## Content Foundation

This app is built upon the complete **Kipsigis Language & Culture Archive** — a two-volume, 38-chapter reference work documenting:

- **Volume One: The Language** — Grammar, dictionary (500+ words), counting, greetings, daily verbs
- **Volume Two: The Culture** — Worldview, ceremonies, social structure, warrior code, proverbs, beer culture, musical heritage
- **Appendices** — Age-Set chronology (1783–Present), 39 clans, self-introduction formula, the *Kaberuret* blessing, and 18 supplementary texts on spirits, curses, witchcraft, and oral traditions
